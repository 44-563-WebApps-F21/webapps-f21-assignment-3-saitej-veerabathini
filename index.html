London

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times. At its centre stand the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations. Across the Thames River, the London Eye observation wheel provides panoramic views of the South Bank cultural complex, and the entire city.

Area of Simple Polygons:

The formula to calculate the area of a regular polygon is, Area = (number of sides × length of one side × apothem)/2, where the value of apothem can be calculated using the formula, Apothem = [(length of one side)/{2 ×(tan(180/number of sides))}]. For more information click here
        double area(const vector& fig) {
            double res = 0;
            for (unsigned i = 0; i < fig.size(); i++) {
                point p = i ? fig[i - 1] : fig.back();
                point q = fig[i];
                res += (p.x - q.x) * (p.y + q.y);
            }
            return fabs(res) / 2;
        }
